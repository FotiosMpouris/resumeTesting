Hard-E: The Future of Sales Automation | Python/Streamlit

Imagine a sales assistant that never sleeps, speaks with the charisma of a seasoned director, and leverages cutting-edge AI to deliver results in real-time. Meet Hard-E—a groundbreaking application I’m building to revolutionize sales operations for the construction industry. Hard-E isn’t just a tool; it’s a game-changer, blending advanced AI technologies to empower businesses with efficiency, insight, and a human touch.

At the heart of Hard-E lies a multi-agent system powered by the OpenAI Agents SDK, enabling seamless task delegation across specialized AI agents. Whether it’s fetching customer data, analyzing job histories, or crafting tailored video scripts, Hard-E orchestrates complex workflows with precision. I’ve harnessed the SDK’s LLM-driven routing to ensure each task is handled by the right agent, making Hard-E scalable and adaptable for future growth.

But what truly sets Hard-E apart is its conversational prowess, thanks to xAI’s Grok 3 Mini Beta. Integrated for natural language interactions, Grok 3 brings a dynamic, human-like dialogue to the table—perfect for engaging clients or guiding users through intricate processes. Whether answering questions about siding materials or pulling job statuses from a CRM, Hard-E delivers responses with clarity and personality, all through a voice-interactive Streamlit interface.

Hard-E also taps into real-time web search capabilities via the Perplexity API, ensuring it stays informed on industry trends—like the latest in construction materials—while maintaining a focus on user needs. From managing customer relationships to generating actionable insights from historical data, Hard-E is built to streamline operations and drive results.

Building Hard-E has been a testament to my passion for leveraging LLMs to solve real-world challenges. By combining the OpenAI Agents SDK for intelligent orchestration, Grok 3 Mini Beta for conversational excellence, and a robust Python/Streamlit framework, I’ve created a platform that’s as innovative as it is practical. If you’re looking for a partner who can harness AI to transform your business—or an expert who thrives on complex builds—I’d love to collaborate and bring that same energy to your projects!
