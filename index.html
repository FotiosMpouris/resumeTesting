OpenCare — Built for the People You Love

I built OpenCare because I was drowning in sticky notes and group texts trying to coordinate care for my parents.

A live, free, production care coordination platform. Family coordinates inside private Care Circles. People who don't use the app — neighbors, caseworkers, siblings in other states — get email notifications and stay in the loop without downloading anything. It's the live MVP for the Poor People App. For the 53 million unpaid family caregivers in the US.

Care Circles

Private workspaces for family, community groups, and care teams. Add members by email. Coordinator controls.

Tasks & Reminders

Recurring schedules from daily to every 5 years. Daily Telegram and email digests. One-tap Google Calendar.

No App Required

Offline contacts get email notifications with full task details. Zero adoption friction for the people you need.

AI Agent API

Full REST API with Bearer auth and safety guardrails. TheGreekClawd already manages tasks via natural language.
