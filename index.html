The Poor People App: Igniting a Financial Revolution!

The Poor People App is the mission to shatter the outdated systems trapping millions in financial limbo, earning too much for aid but too little to thrive. Powered by blockchain technology, voluntary data sharing with industry and municipalities, and the Poor People Coin (PPC); we're building a decentralized platform that empowers users to control and monetize their own data. The Ai revolution is here, an dthe PPA serves as your financial safety net and pathway to blockchain literacy and financial stability. Our strategy builds leverage through user milestones (100K, 1M, 5M users) to create real economic value. Join this grassroots revolution to redefine how people interact with their data in the digital age. The World is changing. We need to change too!
