The Poor People App

A revolutionary platform designed to address class-based economic issues through collective power. Users maintain control of their data, earning tokens by sharing information through a variety of secure self-custody oriented solutions. As automation threatens jobs, PPA serves as your financial safety net, combining data ownership with a tokenized Ethereum economy through our Poor People Coin (PPC). We're building a decentralized marketplace where your data becomes an asset you control, creating leverage through our growing community of users. The first 10,000 subscribers get priority access to earning tokens! Join this grassroots movement to empower middle and lower classes against outdated systems. Explore: https://poorpeopleapp.com
